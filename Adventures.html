What We Offer

Jeep Tours to La Plata

This tour follows alongside the La Plata River all the way down to La Plata City, an old mining town established in 1875. Continuing to the peak of Kennebec Pass at 12,000 feet above sea level, you'll see all the way to Cortez and Telluride!

More Info

Equipment Rental

Fancy doing your own thing? No problem! We have a wide array of different equipment you can rent so that you can go make your own adventure. Just head on down to our centre by Lake Nighthorse to check out what we have.

More Info

NEW: Day Trips to Mesa Verde

This day trip is packed full of adventure! Mesa Verde is one of only 20 World Heritage Sites in North America. Please make sure to bring sturdy walking boots, sunscreen, a reusable water bottle, and most importantly - your camera!

Glamping Pods - Coming Soon!

We are currently working on expanding our experiences to create an all-inclusive adventure. We recently acquired 5 acres of field just outside the town and are beginning construction of 20 brand new ‘glamping pods’ on this site which will be ready in time for next season.
