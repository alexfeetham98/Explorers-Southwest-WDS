About Us

Running tours based in Durango Colorado, which run from March to October these tours we like to see as “mild to mildly adventurous”.

Our main tour, and most popular offer — A guided Jeep tour of the local 'ghost mining towns', these tours have been running since we launched our business off in 2008. This tour takes you up in the mountains located between the towns of Durango and Silverton.
We also offer a fully guided tour to Mesa Verde National Park one hour away.
Rental of equipment is also simething we can sort for you, such as stand up paddle boards (SUP), fishing equipment, and kayaks these are available to acces down at the nearby Lake Nighthorse.

Jeep Tours

Follow the La Plata River to La Plata City, an old mining town established in 1875. Continue to the peak of Kennebec Pass at 12,000 feet above sea level. See all the way to Cortez and Telluride from the top of the pass!

Day Trip to Mesa Verde

Full day - includes a robust packed lunch from the local deli (vegetarian / vegan options available upon request). Return by 5pm. Bring sturdy walking shoes, sun cover, suncream, a reusable water bottle and your camera!
