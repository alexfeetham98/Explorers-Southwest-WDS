About Us

We operate a wide range of adventure tours in and around Durango from
March to October which are “mild to mildly adventurous”

Our Founders, are a husband & wife duo; Skyler and Finely. They established Explorers Southwest back in 2008. Starting out with their own 2 jeeps offering guided tours onto the local ghost mining towns in the mountains between Durango and Silverton.

Over a decade later they have grown the company and now offer a wide range of activities all around the area. This includes guided day trips int the Mesa Verde National Park which is just an hour away from Durango. They also rent out equipment for you to make your own adventures!
