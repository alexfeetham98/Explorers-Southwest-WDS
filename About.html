About Us

We operate a wide range of adventure tours in and around Durango from
March to October which are “mild to mildly adventurous”

Overview

Founders, and husband & wife duo, Skyler and Finely established Explorers Southwest in 2008. Starting out with their 2 jeeps offering guided tours onto the local ghost mining towns in the mountains between Durango and Silverton.

Over a decade later they have grown the company and now offer a wide range of activities all around the area. This includes guided day trips int the Mesa Verde National Park which is just an hour away from Durango. They also rent out equipment for you to make your own adventures!

Ethos

Skyler and Finely were both born and raised in Colorado so naturally adventure sports and a love for the outdoors run deep in their DNA, so it’s no surprise they ended up together. As such, they understand and appreciate that they lice in a location full of sensitive biodiversity. By taking tourists on guided tours, they help reduce the footprint, boost local economy, and keep tourists off delicate ecosystems and restricted land. They can provide a service for tourism while helping to preserve the delicate ecosystem they live in so that it can thrive for future generations to enjoy its immense natural beauty.

Here at Explorers Southwest, we understand that not everyone is a full-blown adrenaline junkie, so we are committed to catering for everyone, no matter what level of adventure they have. Our founders both grew up in families who went outdoors from a young age. Because of this, they understand that providing a positive family vacation experience is vital for the younger generation to learn about, appreciate and explore the great outdoors.
