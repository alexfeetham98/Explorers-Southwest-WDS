Guided Jeep Tours

Journey into the mountains between Durango and Silverton!

Overview

Adventure Level - Mild
Location - The Mountains between Durango and Silverton
Meet Point - Our Base Camp in Durango
Family Friendly - Yes
Season - May to Early October
Numbers - Single up to Groups of 8 people
Duration - ½ Day

Description

Follow the La Plata River to La Plata City, an old mining town established in 1875. Continue to the peak of Kennebec Pass at 12,000 feet above sea level. See all the way to Cortez and Telluride from the top of the pass!

Kids are extra entertained with our “Pan For Gold” scavenger hunt. Find the items on the checklist and get some fool’s gold!

*Please be aware that the roads are not well maintained and not often used which results in a bumpy, bouncy tour! Bring sun cover, suncream, a reusable water bottle, snacks, and your camera!

  • high-alpine meadows

  • Impressive waterfalls and geological features
